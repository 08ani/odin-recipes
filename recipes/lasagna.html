Lasagna Bolognese a la Wander

In this recipe, I will show you how to make good lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Saus
  • Lasagne Leafs
  • Bechamel Saus
  • 3 Celery Sticks
  • 4 Tomatoes
  • 2 Carrots
  • Cheese of Wish

Steps

  1. Boil pasta: Cook in a large pot of salted water per the recipe above.
  2. Cutting: Chop the Celery, Carrots and Tomatoes in small pieces
  3. Prepare meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Add the chopped vegetables to the mix. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  4. Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  5. Layer & bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until browned and bubbly.
